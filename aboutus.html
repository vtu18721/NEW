Path of Sunshine


About Our Organisation

Path of Sunshine is a non-profit organization committed to creating positive change in the world. Through various initiatives and programs, we aim to bring hope and happiness to underprivileged communities. Join us on our journey to make a lasting impact!

We are dedicated to fostering sustainable development, empowering marginalized individuals, and igniting a sense of purpose within communities. Our initiatives span education, healthcare, women's empowerment, and environmental stewardship, all with a shared goal of leaving a lasting, positive footprint on the world. Rooted in values of compassion, collaboration, and transparency, Path of Sunshine is a beacon of optimism that invites you to embark on a journey of meaningful impact. Join us in our quest to radiate warmth, inspire change, and sow the seeds of a brighter future.

Organisational structure

Path of Sunshine believes in multiplying its efforts through collaboration & partnerships that bring together government, non-government, and corporate in a collaborative process for socio-economic development, improving agriculture, environment, health, and women empowerment. Path of Sunshine puts a lot of emphasis on upgrading the skills and capacities of its staff. For this purpose, need-based training is regularly organized

Vision

We aim to create a development revolution for the marginalized and socio-economically weaker sections of society.

Mission

Our mission is the creation of such a society where nobody is deprived of the main current of development. We are determined for the establishment of that society in which everybody possesses ‘Human rights’ with dignity.
